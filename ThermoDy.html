Description of the course


1. Physical Chemistry units

2. Introduction to Thermodynamics
- Definitions: system, surroundings, boundary - Types of systems: isolated, closed, open - Thermodynamic equilibrium

3. Zero's and First Law of Thermodynamics - Internal energy (U) - Work and heat - Conservation of energy - First law equation

4. State Variables and State Functions - Pressure (P), Volume (V), Temperature (T) - Internal energy (U), Enthalpy (H), Helmholtz free energy (A), Gibbs free energy (G) - PV, TS diagrams

5. Second Law of Thermodynamics - Entropy (S) - Reversible and irreversible processes - Entropy of the universe - Clausius and Carnot principles

6. Thermodynamic Cycles - Carnot cycle - Heat engines and refrigerators - Efficiency and performance

7. Thermochemical Calculations - Enthalpy of reaction - Hess's Law - Phase diagrams

8. Chemical Thermodynamics - Gibbs free energy function - Conditions for chemical equilibrium - Standard enthalpy and entropy of reactions - Equilibrium constants
